Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make ricotta mix
  4. Layer the lasagna as such:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parm cheese
    7. Repeat the above until filled, then top with parm
  5. Cover with foil and bake
    • The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375oF. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
  6. Let cool for about 15 minutes
